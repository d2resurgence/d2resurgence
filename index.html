Greetings, stranger. I'm not surprised to see your kind here. Many adventurers have traveled this way since the recent troubles began. No doubt you've heard about the tragedy that befell the town of Tristram. Some say that Diablo, the Lord of Terror, walks the world again...

Resurgence aims to meaningfully expand upon Diablo II's core game while preserving the original experience and atmosphere. These modifications are primarily focused on loot, character build customization, quality-of-life and endgame content.

Both single player and multiplayer are supported in the Legacy (Diablo II: Lord of Destruction) version and our online servers have regular ladder seasons. The Resurrected version of Resurgence is single player only.

Install, play online and keep up to date with our launcher. Manual installation files are available on this repository.

Download

This version is currently in beta, visit our page on the Nexus Mods website for patch download and installation.
